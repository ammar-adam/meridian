# Investor demo film — Meridian

**Runtime:** ~5 minutes (Flow-first)  
**Claim:** Meridian surfaces early Canadian companies from community sources (founders + domains + provenance) before US-centric tools index them — then briefs them against your fund thesis with falsifiable proof packets.

**Prod:** https://meridian-mentor.vercel.app  
**Preflight:** `/demo` or `./scripts/demo-preflight.sh`

---

## Before you hit record (10 min)

1. Paste env vars from `.env.demo` on Vercel → **Redeploy**.
2. Run `./scripts/demo-preflight.sh` — core stack green, corpus ≥ 150.
3. Open `/demo` → **Activate Panache** → **Warm corpus** (optional but helps row count).
4. **Deal Flow** → **Watch this mandate** if not already watching.
5. Confirm Community filter shows rows with **Founders:** lines.
6. Click one row — **Proof PDF** downloads (~2s).
7. Do **not** open Team or claim EverTrace / PitchBook / national registry coverage.

**No pre-cache required** — Flow loads from live corpus; talk over refresh if needed.

---

## Beats

### 1. Deal Flow open (~45s)

> Early Canadian deal flow isn’t in Harmonic. It lives on Velocity, DMZ, CDL cohort pages — fragmented. Meridian structures it with founders, domains, and provenance — matched to your mandate.

Pan **Panache Ventures** in the switcher. Show **Watching mandate**. Filter **Community**. Point at incubator badges + Founders lines. Do not start on the landing page.

### 2. Proof packet (~40s)

Pick one strong row (founders + domain + community source).

> Every row can export a proof packet — dated index checks where we have them, cohort provenance, mandate fit. That’s the receipt funds pay for — not another name search.

**Download proof PDF** or **Copy proof**. Mention falsifiable checks; do not say “we replace PitchBook.”

### 3. CRM handoff (~30s)

Same row or table header.

> Ops teams live in Affinity. Export CSV with proof columns — or copy one company — without retyping from a cohort page.

**Export CSV** or **CRM** copy on one row.

### 4. Brief close (~60s)

Click **Brief** on a domain-ready row. Memo opens after scrape (~2s) and Instant generate (~5–15s). Talk over “Drafting the thesis band.”

Point at fund thesis band → **Pursue**.

> The memo is the delivery vehicle. The moat is community-sourced entities with receipts before they’re indexable.

### 5. Coverage proof (~30s) — optional but strong

Open **Coverage proof** (`/pilot`).

> We measure the wedge — community share, verified index misses, founder reach — not marketing slides.

Cite numbers **on screen** only.

### 6. Foil cut (~25s) — optional

Cut to https://getfundingfromavc.vercel.app: fabricated portfolio company. Back to Meridian: provenance, not inventing from a fund marketing page.

---

## Hard don’ts

- Dead silence on Brief — talk over thesis-band drafting
- Scroll past thin / domain-null incubator stubs
- “We replace PitchBook / Harmonic”
- EverTrace live, national Corporations Canada, WHOIS rush
- Round corpus numbers — say what `/demo` or Pilot shows

---

## Fallback if something breaks on camera

| Issue | On-camera move |
|-------|----------------|
| Flow empty | Switch to Panache, click Watch mandate, Refresh |
| Brief slow | Keep talking — “Instant research + thesis band” |
| PDF fails | Copy proof to clipboard instead |
| Low row count | “Live corpus — we pump daily” + show Pilot stats |
